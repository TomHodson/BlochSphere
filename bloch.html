About

This is a Bloch sphere visualiser - a quantum mechanical simulation of a two state particle under the influence of an external light field. Set an initial position for the Bloch vector, queue up some pulses and press start. If you want free precession, set the Rabi frequency to zero and make the detuning negative. The input boxes support simple math expressions (pi, cos, etc.). The time for a pulse isn't in units of time: rather, it is in angular units (e.g. a pi/2 pulse will rotate the vector by pi/2 around some axis dependent on the Rabi frequency and detuning).
